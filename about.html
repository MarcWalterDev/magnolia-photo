A little about me


Step into my enchanting world at Magnolia Wedding Photography, where I, Marc, a passionate and skilled photographer, am based amidst the breathtaking landscapes of North Wales, eagerly awaiting to weave your love story into timeless imagery. With a deep understanding of the significance of your wedding day, I approach each moment with an unwavering dedication to capturing the essence of your unique bond. Just as the magnolia blooms herald the arrival of spring, my artistry heralds the beginning of your journey as a married couple, preserving the beauty and magic of your special day for generations to come.

Drawing inspiration from the natural splendor that surrounds me, I ensure that every image reflects not just the events of the day, but also the emotions and connections shared between you and your loved ones. From the tender exchange of vows to the jubilant celebrations that follow, my keen eye for detail and my ability to capture fleeting moments of joy and intimacy ensure that your wedding album becomes a cherished keepsake. With Magnolia Wedding Photography, let me guide you through an unforgettable visual narrative, where each photograph tells a story of love, happiness, and the beginning of your happily ever after.
